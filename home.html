Routine Managment

Mobile Application To Aid In Better And Healthier Daily Routines.

Project Description:

An android app based around the idea that when it comes to health and fitness, both mentally and physically, that doing something no matter how small is always better than nothing. One of the main features of this app is to help build a day/night routine to help promote the importance of having something set up and to help you keep on track of your health and fitness goals.

Technologies used:

Android, Firebase, Room Database, Java, Espresso UI Testing and Jetpack compose
